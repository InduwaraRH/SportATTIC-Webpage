Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams where one team bats, trying to score as many runs as possible, while the other team fields and bowls, attempting to get the batsmen out.

Tennis

Tennis is a sport played by two or four players using racquets to hit a ball over a net and into the opponent's court, with the objective of winning points by making the ball land in the opponent's court in such a way that they are unable to return it.
