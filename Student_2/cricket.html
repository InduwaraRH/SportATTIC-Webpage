Cricket

Introduction

Cricket is a game that's played with a bat and ball by two teams of eleven players. It's been around since the 16th century and is now really popular in lots of countries, like India, Australia, and South Africa. The idea is that one team takes turns trying to score points by hitting the ball with the bat and running back and forth between two little wooden sticks called "wickets". The other team tries to stop them from scoring by catching the ball or knocking down the wickets.

History

The first recorded photo of a cricket match taken on 25 July 1857

Cricket is a game that's been around for a really long time! It's believed that kids living in a wooded area in southeast England came up with the game during Saxon or Norman times. The first time anyone wrote about cricket was at the end of the 16th century. Cricket is part of a group of games where players hit a ball with a handheld object, called "club ball." Other games in this group include baseball, golf, hockey, tennis, squash, badminton, and table tennis.
